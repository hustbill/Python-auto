DataPad is a good tool

By: billyzhang2010 原文发布于：2011-02-16 08:57:06

DatePad combines the power of a calendar with the ease of a text editor. DatePad can be used in a large number of ways. It's main function is to store text which is specific to a certain date. You can also store text specific for certain times. It is the perfect way to keep track of whatever you are interested in of your own history. Use DatePad to keep a diary, use it to manage a project, write down your thoughts, keep a record of your dreams..
